Free 3D Furniture Assets for Game Development


Here are some free furniture assets that you can use either while prototyping or directly in your game. The assets are in .obj format and each one has a UV texture in .png format. There is also a folder containing prefabs of the models if you are developing in Unity.

I modeled them in Blender while working on a personal VR project and wanted to share them here for anyone who might find them useful. They are pretty simple models and not the best quality since this was my first 3D modeling project. I would appreciate any feedback you might have on any mistakes I might have made or on how to make it better.

Triangle counts for each model:
- Arm chair: 1540
- Big TV: 610
- Big TV stand: 492
- Book shelf: 4540
- Chair: 214
- Coffee table: 484
- Computer chair: 1080
- Cupboard: 772
- Dinner table: 484
- Double bed: 1468
- Double sofa: 2568
- Single bed: 1194
- Small TV and its stand: 1038
- Tripple sofa: 3596
