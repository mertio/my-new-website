Apple II (1977)

After the success of the Apple One Computer, Steve Wozniak got excited and decided to build the second computer, Apple II. This computer would be the one that would make Apple take off. When it first came out in 1977 it was promoted as "an extraordinary computer for ordinary people". It was a refined version of the Apple I motherboard, with added color, memory capacity, eight expansion slots, and an attractive beige case with a built-in brown keyboard. () The things in the computer that stood out at the time compared to its rivals was the nice graphical display and the user friendly design.
