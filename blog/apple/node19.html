The iPhone

In the year 2007, a new smartphone entered the market. Instead of the usual keypad, this phone had a touchscreen. It had computer-like capabilities that have never been seen on another device. The original iPhone did not have any third party apps, GPS, or video recording.


After the success and acceptance the first iPhone received, what really made people see its potential was the model that came out in 2008. Along with this model they launched the App Store for the first time and it also supported 3G, a mobile communications standard that allowed mobile phones to access the internet much faster than before.
After the 3G and the 3GS model(which was basically an upgraded version of the 3G model) came the iPhone 4. It had a newer, more modern design and its screen was much better than the previous models. It had the first ever Retina display. It was also the first iPhone to have a front facing camera. It can be said that this was the most significant model.()
As the years went by, each model after that came out was slightly better than the previous one and the iPhone slowly became the most popular smartphone series in the world along with Samsung. The one that most recently came out at the time of writing is the iPhone X, which is seen as the best smartphone on the market along with the Samsung Galaxy Note 8.
