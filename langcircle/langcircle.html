Welcome to Lang Circle!

Lang Circle is a pen pal app for language learning that allows you to make friends from all over the world and practice your language skills. You can find users that speak the language you are learning and can have one-on-one chats with them. There is a social media feed where you can share your life in another language and native speakers can correct your mistakes. There is a "Guessing Game" where a player picks a word and the other tries to guess what it is. Create an account now and become one of its first users!
